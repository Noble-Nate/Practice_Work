About Me

Welcome, I just wanted to give you guys a overview of who I am and what I do. So I'm from Houston, Texas, and have recently moved to Pico Rivera, California; which is convenity located between Los Angeles and Orange County.

My two favorite things to do in my free time are to watch movies (all types), and to play PC games on my custom gaming desktop (one of my prized possession).Of course I'm not just a indoor type of person, when the weather is nice I enjoy spending time with my family outdoors by either fishing and camping or practicing my favorite sport which is archery. I enjoy archery so much that I actually trained in order to become a level 3 National US Olympic Certified Instructor.

I'm also a very caring and concered individual, which is why I activily participate in volunteer opputunities for nonprofits. I particulary enjoy volunteering for organizations that work with orphans and the foster system, and to a larger extent youth in general; because I feel that I'm actually affecting a real problem that is not commonly knowledged. At the moment I am actually working on multiple pro-bono (free) projects for some organizations that work in that particular area.
