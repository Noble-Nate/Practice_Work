Game Programmer

I’m currently working towards my Bachelors Degree at the Art Institute of Los Angeles in Santa Monica.

I have a passion for gaming, so the Tech I chose and was accepted into was Computer Science. In my Senior Year, I was accepted into the Game Programming Tech.
